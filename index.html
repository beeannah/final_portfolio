About me

Hi! I'm Breanna. I am a product designer based in San Diego. As a designer, my focus lies in designing with the user in mind and creating for the greater good. My focus lies at the intersection of technology and design. As a designer, I aim to develop products that both seamlessly communicates to and meets user needs, one step at a time.

I am originally from Orange County and am currently a 3rd year student at UC San Diego majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction. Alongside design, I am a multi-media crafter who enjoys exploring different forms of mediums from digital illustrations to graphic design.
